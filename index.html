~ About the Author ~

Emrys Byrne writes epic fantasy in the old tradition — big stories, flawed people, and magic that leaves marks.

He grew up on Tolkien, cut his teeth on George R.R. Martin, and keeps returning to Ursula K. Le Guin when he needs to remember why any of this matters.

The Shield of the People is his debut novel.

Epic Fantasy · First in a Series

The Shield of the People

Thadeon of the Aldeaaris is the general who keeps a fractured alliance from war. His brother Thamred is the young paladin with magic Thadeon will never have. Delaine is the mage Thadeon loves, the outsider his family despises, and the closest thing Thamred has to a sister.

When the queen is murdered, Thadeon watches religious fervor sweep through Balneor like fire through dry grass. The holy man his family trusted for decades is fanning the flames, and the alliance Thadeon fought to hold together is already fracturing. Sending Thamred and Delaine to safety, he rides to prevent a war that's already closer than anyone knows.

But safety is an illusion, and the war Thadeon hoped to prevent has already begun.
